Project Design (CSCI 497)

Master the planning phase by crafting compelling problem statements, comprehensive requirements specifications, and detailed project proposals that clearly define your vision and scope.

🔨

Project Construction (CSCI 498)

Build significant software solutions from your design specifications, implement rigorous test plans, and develop professional-quality code that meets real-world requirements.

🎤

Project Defense (CSCI 499)

Present your completed work to faculty and peers through public presentations and comprehensive final reports. Demonstrate your problem-solving abilities and technical expertise.

Ethics & Professional Practice

Make informed judgments about your work as a computer scientist based on legal and ethical principles. Think critically about the broader consequences of your technology.
